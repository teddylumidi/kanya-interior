02 / Services Directory

Our Technical Specialties

Highly engineered wall preparation, decorative architectural layouts, flooring finishes, and custom cabinetry setups executed with precision.

Skimming & Wall Finishing

Precision multi-layered undercoats to achieve zero-defect smooth walls, preparing surfaces perfectly for luxury paint or architectural wainscoting.

Painting Services

Premium interior and exterior coatings utilizing professional-grade, long-lasting emulsions with beautiful color-matching expertise.

Wallmaster Installations

Highly resilient, weather-proof textured cladding designed to defend external facades while offering modern visual elements.

Gypsum Installation

State-of-the-art false ceilings, double-tiered bulkhead channels, integrated LED ambient voids, and flawless plaster boards.

Tile Installation

Precision porcelain, ceramic, and marble flooring tiling alignment with clean waterproofing and premium epoxy grouting.

SPC Flooring

Premium Stone Plastic Composite waterproof floorboards laid elegantly across your space with protective sound barriers.

Kitchen Cabinet Design

Highly customizable modern kitchen cabinetry layout including silent soft-close hinges, quartz worktops, and modular space efficiency.

Bespoke Wardrobes

Tailor-made floor-to-ceiling sliding or hinged wardrobes, optimizing internal configurations to support complete organization.

Wainscoting & Panels

Classic and contemporary custom solid wood moldings and symmetrical panels providing elegant structural visual highlights.
